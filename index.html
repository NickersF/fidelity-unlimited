POLICIES

  • Sessions begin at the time booked by client, and continue until the final load out.

  • A 50% per day deposit is required at time of booking.

  • Your deposit will not be refunded if the session is cancelled with less than 3 weeks’ notice; exceptions will be made for major emergencies.

  • Payment accepted in cash, credit card, money order and certified check.

  • Payment is due at the end of the session.

  • Consecutive day blocks are mandatory if gear or mixes are to be left set up overnight.

  • Transfers, backup of audio, and session file transfer time is billable at normal studio rates.

  • All mixes, CD-Rs, hard drives, master tapes, etc. shall remain the property of Fidelity Unlimited LLC until all invoices are paid in full.

  • Fidelity Unlimited is not legally or financially responsible for items brought into or left on the premises. This includes master tapes, drives, etc.

  • Tape house reels are to be used by project in a timely manner agreed upon by client and studio. If not upheld a storage fee of $100 a week will be charged to client.
